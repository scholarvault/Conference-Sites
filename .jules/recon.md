## 2026-04-30 - SDG Branding as Legitimacy Shield
**Source:** Academic Research Library (academicresearchlibrary.com)
**Pattern Found:** Prominent use of United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) icons and branding to categorize proceedings, creating a false sense of institutional prestige or global endorsement.
**Reliability:** High confidence that this is an aesthetic signal used to mask low-quality or generic conferences.
**Watch For:** Other platforms adopting official-looking non-governmental or UN branding (SDG grids, "Global Goals" badges) without actual affiliation.

## 2026-04-30 - The Generic Conference "Tour" Pattern
**Source:** World Research Library (worldresearchlibrary.org)
**Pattern Found:** Multiple instances of identically named generic conferences (e.g., "IRAJ INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE", "Researchfora International Conference", "Academics World International Conference") scheduled across different major global cities (Kuala Lumpur, Tokyo, Shanghai, Hangzhou) within days of each other.
**Reliability:** High confidence as a high-risk signal. Legitimate academic societies rarely run the exact same conference in multiple cities simultaneously or consecutively.
**Watch For:** Organizers like IRAJ, Researchfora, and Academics World operating these mass-produced "tour" events.
