Conference Awards

Recognizing excellence in research, innovation, and contribution to AI in Healthcare.

Award Categories

What You Can Win

🥇

Best Paper Award

Awarded to the highest-scoring original research paper across all tracks. Includes certificate and feature in conference proceedings.

Open for Nomination
🌟

Young Researcher Award

For PhD scholars and early-career researchers (under 35) demonstrating exceptional innovation in AI healthcare research.

Open for Nomination

Excellence in AI Healthcare

For seasoned researchers with significant contributions to the field of AI in medicine and clinical care.

Open for Nomination

Best Presentation Award

Judges evaluate clarity, delivery, and Q&A performance during virtual presentations. Announced live at conference.

Open for Nomination
💡

Innovation in Clinical AI

Recognizes research translating AI from theory to clinical practice — actual deployments, RCTs, or pilot studies in hospitals.

Open for Nomination

International Contribution Award

For international delegates whose research contributes to global health equity and AI accessibility in low-resource settings.
