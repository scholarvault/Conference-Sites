What We Cover

ICAHCR 2026 welcomes original research, review articles, and case studies across all intersections of AI and clinical healthcare.

AI in Diagnostics

Machine learning for medical imaging, radiology AI, pathology automation, and clinical decision support systems.

Drug Discovery & Genomics

AI-driven drug repurposing, precision medicine, CRISPR applications, and personalized genomic therapy.

Telemedicine & Digital Health

Remote patient monitoring, IoT wearables, AI-enhanced telehealth platforms, and virtual care delivery.

Hospital Operations AI

Bed management, predictive discharge planning, AI in EHR systems, and hospital workflow optimization.

Clinical Research & Trials

AI for patient recruitment, trial design optimization, regulatory compliance, and real-world evidence.

Health Data Privacy & Ethics

Federated learning, health data governance, AI bias in medicine, and ethical frameworks for clinical AI.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ICAHCR 2026. Can't find the answer? Contact us →

Is ICAHCR 2026 fully virtual?
Yes. ICAHCR 2026 is a 100% virtual conference. All sessions, keynotes, Q&A, and networking happen online. You can attend from anywhere in the world with no travel required.
What is the ScholarVault Verified badge?
The ScholarVault badge (PLATINUM · 92/100) is awarded after an independent 18-point forensic audit covering peer review quality, fee transparency, indexing, refund policies, organizing committee integrity, and more. It cannot be purchased — only earned. Learn more →
Can I submit a paper and register at the same time?
You can submit your abstract independently through the Submit Paper page. Registration is a separate step. Authors typically register after paper acceptance, but early registration is also accepted.
What file formats are accepted for paper submission?
Abstracts and full papers can be submitted as PDF or DOCX files (max 10 MB). Speaker photos should be JPEG or PNG (min 400×400 px). All uploads are handled securely through our submission portal.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes. All registered delegates receive a digital participation certificate. Presenting authors receive a separate author/presentation certificate. Co-authors are listed and receive co-author certificates. These are issued digitally within 7 days after the conference.
What is the early bird deadline?
Early bird pricing is available until July 15, 2026. Register before this date to save on your conference fee. Student and ScholarVault Gold discounts can be combined with early bird pricing.
What is the refund policy?
100% refund if cancelled before July 1, 2026. 50% refund if cancelled between July 1–31, 2026. No refund after August 1, 2026. Full details on our Refund Policy page →
Will proceedings be published with an ISBN?
Yes. Conference proceedings will be published with an ISBN and made available as a digital PDF to all registered delegates. Authors who present papers will have their work indexed in the proceedings.
