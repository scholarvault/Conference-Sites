ICAHCR 2026 — August 22–23

About the Conference

The International Conference on AI and Healthcare Research (ICAHCR) 2026 is a peer-reviewed, ScholarVault–verified academic summit bringing together researchers, clinicians, data scientists, and health innovators to advance the science of AI-driven healthcare.

Who We Are

What is ICAHCR?

ICAHCR 2026 is organized under the ScholarVault Conference Series — a credentialed initiative to provide legitimate, non-predatory academic conference infrastructure to the global research community.

The conference is 100% virtual, enabling researchers from 50+ countries to participate without travel cost or visa hurdles. Every accepted paper undergoes double-blind peer review by domain experts.

The 2026 edition focuses on the convergence of clinical practice and AI innovation — from frontline diagnostics to complex drug discovery — shaping the next era of evidence-based medicine.
