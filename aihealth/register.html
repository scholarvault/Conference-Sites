Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in each registration category?
All categories include live session access and a digital certificate. Faculty/Researcher adds session recordings and keynote Q&A. Student/Scholar is cost-effective with full virtual access. Listener is for audit-only attendance. Co-Author is for non-presenting co-authors who need certification.
Can I register with an international card / PayPal?
Yes. Payments are processed through Dodo Payments which accepts all major international credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets. USD pricing is shown by switching the currency toggle above.
What is the ScholarVault Gold Member discount?
ScholarVault Gold members get 15% off any registration tier, a special "Gold Delegate" certificate, and priority support. Enter your SV Gold Member ID (format: SVG-XXXXX) in the Gold Member section on this page to apply the discount automatically.
When will I receive my joining link?
A confirmation email is sent immediately after payment. The conference joining link and calendar invite will be sent 3 days before the event (August 19, 2026). Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Can I register as a group for my institution?
Yes. For groups of 5 or more delegates, email conferences@scholarvault.in for special institutional pricing and a bulk registration form.
