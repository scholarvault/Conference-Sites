What Is This Badge?

Earned Through India's Most Rigorous Audit

India has thousands of conferences — many predatory. They collect fees, promise Scopus indexing, deliver nothing. Researchers now check before they submit. The ScholarVault Verified Badge is an independent answer to this crisis.
