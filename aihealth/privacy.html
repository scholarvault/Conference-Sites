Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

When you register, submit a paper, apply to speak, or fill any form on this website, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Institutional affiliation, designation, country
  • Research paper content and metadata
  • Payment information (processed securely by Dodo Payments — we do not store card numbers)
  • IP address, browser type, and event analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Send registration confirmations, joining links, and receipts
  • Process paper review and acceptance notifications
  • Publish your name in conference proceedings (if accepted)
  • Send conference updates if you subscribed
  • Improve our website through aggregated analytics (Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity)

3. Data Storage

All form data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS with AES-256 encryption at rest. File uploads (papers, photos) are stored in Supabase Storage. We retain records for up to 5 years for academic proceedings purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Dodo Payments — payment processing. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymized website usage analytics.
  • Microsoft Clarity — heatmap and session recording (anonymized).

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@scholarvault.in. We will respond within 7 business days.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and session state only. No advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@scholarvault.in
