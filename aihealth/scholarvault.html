About ScholarVault

The academic integrity platform protecting researchers from predatory journals and conferences — while organizing legitimate, high-trust events.

The Platform

What is ScholarVault?

ScholarVault is a SaaS platform and academic services organization based in India. Our mission is dual-faceted: first, we help researchers identify and avoid predatory journals and conferences through our intelligent forensic verification engine. Second, we organize and host legitimate academic conferences under the ScholarVault Conference Series banner.

The platform has been used by researchers from IITs, NITs, AIIMSs, and universities across India, Southeast Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. We operate on the principle that academic trust should be verifiable, not assumed.
