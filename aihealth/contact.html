Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant answers to common queries before reaching out.

How long does it take to get a response?
Our team responds within 24 business hours. For urgent matters related to registration payment or final submission deadlines, please mention "URGENT" in your message subject. Registered delegates with ScholarVault Gold membership get priority 2-hour support.
I made a payment but haven't received confirmation — what should I do?
First check your spam/junk folder. If not received within 2 hours of payment, email conferences@scholarvault.in with your payment transaction ID and we'll manually send your confirmation immediately.
Can I request a group discount for my institution?
Yes. Institutions registering 5 or more delegates are eligible for a group discount. Contact us via the form above selecting "Group / Institutional Registration" and our team will arrange a customised pricing quote.
How do I request a refund?
Email us with your registration ID and reason for cancellation. Refunds are processed per our Refund Policy: 100% before July 1, 50% from July 1–31, none after August 1, 2026.
