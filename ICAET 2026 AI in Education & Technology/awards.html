Awards & Recognition

ICAES 2026 honours the best research and contributors across the conference programme.

🏆
Best Paper Award
Awarded to the most outstanding paper in originality, methodology, and impact. Includes cash prize and certificate.
🎤
Best Presentation Award
Recognises the researcher who best communicates their work in the live virtual session. Judged by committee.
⭐
Outstanding Reviewer
Honours reviewers providing the most thorough, constructive, and timely peer reviews throughout the process.
🌱
Best Sustainability Impact
Special award for the paper with the highest projected real-world sustainability and SDG impact.
🎓
Best Student Paper
For papers where the primary author is currently enrolled as a student. Certificate, recognition, and prize.
🔬
Track Best Papers ×8
One Best Paper award per research track — ensuring every specialisation area receives dedicated recognition.
