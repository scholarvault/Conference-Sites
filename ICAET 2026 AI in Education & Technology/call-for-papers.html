Submission Guidelines

Submission Guidelines

📄 Paper Length

Full papers: 6–10 pages in IEEE/Springer format. Short papers: 4–6 pages. Posters: 2 pages.

📐 Format

Submit in PDF format. Use the provided template (available in Downloads). Submissions must be in English.

🔍 Review Process

Double-blind peer review by at least 2 subject-matter reviewers. Evaluation on originality, significance, and presentation.

✅ Originality

All submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere. Plagiarism checks are run on all papers.
