About ICAES 2026

International Conference on AI for Environmental Sustainability

Our Mission

ICAES 2026 exists at the urgent intersection of two defining challenges of our time: the accelerating climate crisis and the rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence. Our mission is to build a global community of researchers who are actively deploying AI solutions to environmental problems — not merely theorising about them.

From satellite-based deforestation detection to neural-network-driven energy grid optimisation, from precision irrigation algorithms to automated biodiversity monitoring — the work being done at this intersection is transformative. ICAES 2026 provides the platform to share, scrutinise, and amplify this work.

As a 100% virtual conference, we deliberately break down geographic and financial barriers to participation, ensuring that researchers from all countries — including those in the Global South most affected by climate change — can contribute their perspectives and findings.

Conference Themes

AI-Powered Climate Solutions
Machine learning models for climate projection, extreme weather early warning, and carbon cycle simulation.
Sustainable Agriculture Intelligence
Precision farming, soil microbiome AI, water stress detection, and supply chain carbon tracking.
Green Energy Systems
Renewable forecasting, smart grid management, battery optimisation, and demand-side AI.
Ecological Monitoring
Species recognition, habitat mapping, ocean health AI, and forest cover change detection.
Circular Economy & Waste
AI for waste classification, recycling optimisation, industrial symbiosis, and product lifecycle analysis.
Environmental Policy & Governance
AI tools for policy impact modelling, climate finance, ESG reporting, and carbon market integrity.
