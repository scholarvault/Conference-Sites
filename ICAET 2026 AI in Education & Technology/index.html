About the Conference

Where AI Meets the Planet

ICAES 2026 is an international virtual conference dedicated to the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Environmental Sustainability. As climate change accelerates, AI offers transformative tools — from satellite-based deforestation detection to neural-network-driven energy optimisation.

Our conference unites researchers, environmental scientists, policy makers, and technologists to share peer-reviewed work across 8 specialised research tracks — spanning climate modelling, smart agriculture, ocean monitoring, green computing, and more.

Research Tracks

8 Specialised Research Areas

ICAES 2026 covers the full spectrum of AI-driven environmental research — from climate modelling to green computing.

Climate AI & Modelling
Neural networks for climate prediction, extreme weather forecasting, sea-level rise simulation, and atmospheric data analysis.
Track 1
Precision Agriculture
AI-driven crop monitoring, soil health analysis, drone-based field inspection, irrigation optimisation, and food security.
Track 2
Smart Energy Grids
Reinforcement learning for grid management, renewable energy forecasting, demand response, EV integration, and microgrids.
Track 3
Biodiversity Monitoring
Computer vision for species identification, acoustic AI for wildlife monitoring, satellite imagery analysis, and ecosystem mapping.
Track 4
Pollution Control & Sensing
IoT-AI integration for air/water quality, real-time pollution mapping, industrial emissions monitoring, and remediation planning.
Track 5
Ocean & Water Systems
Autonomous underwater vehicles, ocean temperature AI, marine biodiversity, coral reef monitoring, and water resource management.
Track 6
Carbon Accounting & LCA
AI for lifecycle assessment, automated carbon footprint tracking, supply chain emissions, carbon credit verification, and net-zero pathways.
Track 7
Green Computing & AI Ethics
Energy-efficient AI architectures, sustainable data centres, federated learning for conservation, and ethical AI governance for environment.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICAES 2026?
ICAES 2026 — International Conference on AI for Environmental Sustainability — is a 100% virtual academic conference bringing together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and environmental science. It is organised by ScholarVault and is fully virtual, open to researchers from all countries.
When and where is ICAES 2026?
ICAES 2026 is scheduled for Q4 2026. Exact dates will be announced soon — register your interest above to be notified first. The conference is 100% virtual with no in-person attendance required.
Who can submit papers?
Anyone — faculty, PhD scholars, postdoctoral researchers, industry professionals, and independent researchers — can submit. Papers must be original, unpublished work relevant to one of the 8 research tracks. Interdisciplinary submissions bridging AI and environmental fields are especially encouraged.
Are proceedings indexed?
Accepted papers are published in ISBN-registered conference proceedings, providing a citable, permanent record. Additional indexing details will be announced as the conference approaches.
How do I know this is a legitimate conference?
