Organising committee

ISIAI-SGS 2026 is being assembled through an interdisciplinary committee spanning programme strategy, peer review, speaker curation, and conference operations.

Programme Committee

Shapes track direction, review quality, and the editorial character of the conference.

Track Leads

Reviewer Network
